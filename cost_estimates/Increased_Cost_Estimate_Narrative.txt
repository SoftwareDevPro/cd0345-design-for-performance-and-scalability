Given an increase in the budget (twice over), and seeking to add redundancy, and improve peformance, a number of things can be done. Adding redundancy can be achieved adding a second region (US West - Oregon in this case).  Additionally Route 53 is added to perform health checks, and given enough threshold failures can switch traffic to healthy instances of the application.  RDS storage was increased as well because given its a social media application; its likely to need more then 30 GB.  Instance types were increased for the application servers as they are more likely to need the processing power in low and high user traffic.
